💌 A Letter For You

Dear Mamata,

Good morning, my love. I hope this message finds you well and fills your heart with warmth and happiness. You always ask me where I was busy, so here is my answer — I was busy to sharpen my skills. ❤️

I just want you to know that you are the most important person in my life. Every single day, I find more reasons to appreciate you, admire you, and love you even more than before. Your smile, your care, your presence — everything about you makes my life brighter and more beautiful.

I also want to sincerely apologize for all the times I hurt you, knowingly or unknowingly. From now onward, I promise I will try my best to become a better person for you and make you happy in every possible way.

Maybe these words are simple, but they are coming straight from my heart. I truly hope you like this letter, and I hope you will always stay with me through every moment of life.

I love you so much, and I will always love you with all my heart.

With my heartfelt love to my Korean nurse, Mamata Mahato. 💖

—your long distance love

Aaron
